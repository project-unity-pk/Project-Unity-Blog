Founders
  • Zain Khurram

    Having been overweight in the past, Zain deeply and personally understands the social and physical ramifications of Pakistan's culture of body shaming. Now, having lost weight, he hopes to educate people about the dangers of body shaming through immaculately editted instagram posts, teaching people how to create a body positive environment, and much more.

  • Ahmed Imran

    Going through a long and painstaking journey of losing weight, Ahmed has been at the forefront of body-shaming slurs towards his lose skin and unnatural body. Through, project unity, he hopes to ensure that this does not happen to anyone else. Now spending his entire day at the gym, he has learnt to love himself and hopes to help others do the same.

  • Ibrahim Zaheer

    Ibrahim is a man of above-average size with a burly figure. His body does not match society's expectations of perfections. Naturally, this makes him insecure but he is determined to change that through learning to love himself, and more significantly, through reaching out to the community and educating his peers.

  • Ibraheem Saqib

    Being underweight for most of his life, Ibraheem has been a victim of the body-shaming culture. Through this project, he hopes to prevent this from happening to anyone else. In this effort, he aims to reach and educate as many people as possible through several mediums, writing being one of them.
